habatat

Armadillos live in temperate and warm habitats, including rain forests, grasslands, and semi-deserts. Because of their low metabolic rate and lack of fat stores, cold is their enemy, and spates of intemperate weather can wipe out whole populations.

care

Armadillos need room to forage and dig, and they will not be happy unless you allow them to do so. A large outdoor enclosure such as a fenced-in yard or dog pen should work. Be aware that an active armadillo just might dig its way out of a pen, so keep an eye on it.

likes and dislikes

Armadillos generally dislike most anything that poses a threat or is uncomfortable. This includes loud noises, unfamiliar smells, and overly bright lights. In addition, they are naturally shy and timid creatures, so they can be deterred by large crowds or unexpected movement.
